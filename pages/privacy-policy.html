Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

To provide our retro arcade gaming services, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and encrypted password for user authentication
  • Game Progress: High scores, levels completed, and gameplay statistics
  • Subscription Data: Payment processing through Stripe (we don't store payment details)
  • Technical Data: Login times, failed login attempts for security purposes

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide access to premium games and features
  • Save and sync your game progress across sessions
  • Process subscription payments securely
  • Send password reset emails when requested
  • Protect against unauthorized access with rate limiting

Data Security

Your security is our priority:

  • Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt with 12 salt rounds
  • JWT tokens for secure session management
  • Rate limiting to prevent brute force attacks
  • Account lockout after 5 failed login attempts
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

Third-Party Services

  • Stripe: Payment processing for subscriptions (see Stripe's privacy policy)
  • Email Service: Gmail for sending password reset emails only

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request account deletion by contacting us.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data and game progress
  • Update your email address
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Manage your subscription through Stripe's customer portal

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal browser storage for:

  • JWT authentication tokens (stored in localStorage)
  • Basic game preferences
  • No third-party tracking or analytics cookies

Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests, please contact us or email us directly.
